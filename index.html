Today's facts.

  • The Solar System spans about 287.46 billion kilometers or 179 billion miles across, but 99.8% of its mass is in the Sun alone.

  • The Sun's core reaches 15 million degrees celsius - hot enough to fuse hydrogen into helium and power the entire solar system.

  • The Earth is the only known planet where water exists naturally as a solid, liquid and gas.

  • The Moon is slowly drifting away from Earth at 3.8cm per year, affecting tides and Earth's rotation.
